Privacy Policy

Information Gathering and Usage

During Bitbucket PlantUML Viewer installation, users grant access to the add-on to read repositories. The access is needed to read the content of a file in order to display it on the Bitbucket source view page. No cloning operations are executed.

Bitbucket PlantUML Viewer uses local tracking to the developer that allows us to better understand our users and improve the services we offer. The information gathered is non-personally identifiable.

Data Storage

Bitbucket PlantUML Viewer uses third party vendor and hosting partner, Firebase Hosting, to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Bitbucket PlantUML Viewer. Although I own the code, databases, and all rights to the Bitbucket PlantUML Viewer platform, you retain all rights to your data.

Disclosure

The information we collect is used to improve the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested or when we have your permission.
